How to Use VideoSign

1. Start Meeting

Join your secure, online meeting room.

2. Upload a Document

Upload a document to your meeting room.

3. View Documents

View and discuss the document with your participants.

4. Sign Document

Sign the document by using either a saved, drawn or typed signature.

5. Confirm Signature

Verify that your signature, name and date are correct.

6. Place Signature

Place your signature at the desired position on the document.
